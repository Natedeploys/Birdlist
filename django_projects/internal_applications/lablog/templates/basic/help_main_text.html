Hello and Welcome to Lablog!

You seem to be new around here. Let me briefly explain to you what Lablog is:
  • Lablog is like your old paper labbook where you want to write down all your ideas, experiments, attempts and results.
  • However, Lablog is more than this. With Lablog you not only write a diary about your scientific work, but Lablog let's you actually find your notes again! You can tag your entries, search them and find them. Amazing, isn't it?
  • Lablog is also tightly integrated with your favorite animal database - no need to copy / paste around data about animals anymore.

Let's get started

In order to use Lablog you only need to know a few things:
  • In Lablog everything starts with a Project. A Project can be anything from "get my life organized" to "take over the world". A Project bundles all the little missions that are necessary to finally one day accomplish your Project goal.
  • Experiments are all the little missions that you attempt in order to accomplish your Project goal. Experiments themselves consist of Events.
  • Every Experiment follows a certain Protocol. You can define as many as you want and a single Experiment can even built upon multiple Protocols.
There are many more functions, but why don't you go ahead and create a new Project and a new Experiment.

Beware of slugs!

One last word of advice:
  • Lablog is full of so called slugs. A slug is a "short text used in a URL to identify and describe a resource". Instead of having long, confusing URLs, why not use something meaningful? What is easier to read and interpret "/{{username}}/experiment/43932/" or "/{{username}}/experiment/my_super_experiment_with_electroshocks/"? We believe the latter.
  • The only thing that you need to know about slugs is that they may not contain any whitespace or non standard letters. Slugs need to be "URL friendly" - but Lablog will help you with this.
